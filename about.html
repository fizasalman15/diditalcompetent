Overview of Solar Systems and CCTV Cameras

.Solar Systems: Solar systems generate electricity using sunlight as a renewable energy source. They consist of solar panels that convert sunlight into electricity, inverters that convert it to usable AC power, and sometimes batteries for energy storage. Solar systems are environmentally friendly and increasingly popular due to declining costs and incentives.
CCTV Cameras: CCTV cameras are used for video surveillance and security purposes. They capture and record video footage, acting as a deterrent and aiding in investigations. They come in various types and offer features like high-definition recording, night vision, and remote access. CCTV systems can be integrated with other security systems and connected to computer networks for remote monitoring.
